Sudoku Solver By BuddyLich

What is this?

This is a web page that can solve sudoku by using backtracking algorithm. It can also display the process solving the sudoku puzzle.

How to use it?

If you want to solve the sudoku puzzle instantly, you can just fill in the puzzle and click "Solve Instantly". As long as the puzzle is solvable, the result will appear within a second.

You can also see the backtracking process by selecting display speed and click "Display Solving Process". For a medium level Sudoku puzzle, it usually takes about 10 seconds by the speed of 1000 steps per second. It is not recommended to display an extremely difficult puzzle since that will take a lot of RAM.

You can also generate a Sudoku puzzle by clicking "Create Sample Puzzle" button. The puzzle is hard coded at the moment. I might add a feature to generate random puzzle in future.
