Sudoku Solver by Buddylich

What is this?

This is a web page that solves sudoku puzzle by using a backtracking algorithm. You can also choose the speed of the solving process.

You can generate a Sudoku puzzle by clicking the "Create Sample Puzzle" button. The generated puzzle is hardcoded at the moment.
